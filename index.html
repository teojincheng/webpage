Booking/Appointment Page

A web app that simulates a user selecting date and time for making an appointment/booking. The app is hosted on Google App Engine and uses data stored in Google Cloud Datastore.

While a booking is being made, the timeslot is put on hold and not selectable by others.

The holding time is indefinite until user confirms or cancels the selection.

Confirmed booking and on-hold timeslot is updated every 1 second

Walking App
